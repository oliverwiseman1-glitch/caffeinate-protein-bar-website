Our Story

Why We Created BuzzBar

The personal journey behind Sweden's newest protein bar revolution.

It Started with a Simple Problem

Like many busy professionals, we were tired of the 3 PM energy crash. Coffee worked for a few hours, energy drinks left us jittery, and most protein bars tasted like cardboard. We wanted something that actually worked - clean energy, real nutrition, and great taste.

The Search for Better

After months of experimenting in our Stockholm kitchen, testing ingredients from Swedish suppliers, and countless iterations, BuzzBar was born. As brothers with a shared vision, we combined our expertise to create something truly special. We use only premium protein sources, natural caffeine from green coffee beans, and absolutely no artificial nonsense.
